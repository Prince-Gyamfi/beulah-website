Pension Fund for Rural Kingdom Workers

Many rural pastors and evangelists in Ghana labor faithfully for decades without any formal retirement benefits. At Beulah Foundation, we believe that no Kingdom worker should spend their old age in lack or neglect. Our Pension Fund was created to provide sustainable, long-term financial security for these selfless servants.

Through consistent donations and wise stewardship, Beulah Foundation manages a growing pension fund dedicated to supporting retired ministers. The fund ensures that faithful men and women who have given their lives to the Gospel can enjoy dignity, comfort, and peace during their later years.

Securing Their Future, Honoring Their Past

The Pension Fund is not just a financial program — it’s an act of gratitude, restoring honor to those who have carried the Gospel to hard-to-reach places, often with little earthly reward.

Play Video

Our vision is to expand the Pension Fund so that every rural Kingdom worker who has served faithfully will have a guaranteed safety net for life. We are building a culture where the labor of love is never forgotten, and every minister retires with dignity.

Program Highlights

The Pension Fund for Rural Kingdom Workers includes:

  • Monthly pension stipends for retired ministers
  • Emergency health and welfare support
  • Legacy awards for long-serving ministers
  • Financial management and transparency reports
  • Expansion plans to cover all regions of Ghana

Supporting faithful Kingdom workers in rural Ghana through housing, education, and monthly care.
