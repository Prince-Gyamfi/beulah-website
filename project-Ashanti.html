Ashanti Region Outreach

Restoring Hope in Ashanti Region: Housing, Care & Evangelism

Ashanti Region has always been a key focus area for Beulah Foundation’s mission. Home to many faithful Kingdom workers who served tirelessly for decades in rural communities, Ashanti presents both opportunity and deep need. Through a combination of housing construction, monthly support, welfare care, and rural evangelism, Beulah Foundation has worked to restore dignity, hope, and sustainability to the lives of many retired ministers and their families in this region.

In Ashanti, we completed the construction and dedication of permanent homes for retired pastors who otherwise faced aging without proper shelter. These houses, built under the Beulah Housing Project, are handed over with full ownership in the names of the beneficiaries, a testament to their lifelong service. Simultaneously, Beulah’s Monthly Support Program ensures that retired ministers in remote towns receive financial aid, basic welfare support, and spiritual encouragement. Alongside these efforts, our Evangelism and Rural Outreach team conducted open-air crusades and village missions, reaching new souls and strengthening local churches in the rural parts of Ashanti. Every project executed here combines physical restoration with spiritual renewal, transforming both the lives of individuals and entire communities.

Supporting faithful Kingdom workers in rural Ghana through housing, education, and monthly care.
