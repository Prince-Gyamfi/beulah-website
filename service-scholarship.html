Scholarships for Ministers' Children

Beulah Foundation believes that the children of Kingdom workers deserve every opportunity to succeed, just as their parents sowed seeds into the lives of others. Through our Scholarship Program, we support the education of children whose parents served faithfully in rural missions but face financial hardship in providing schooling.

We provide scholarships for tuition, school supplies, and other educational needs, enabling these children to attend quality schools and pursue a brighter future. By empowering them through education, Beulah extends the legacy of service and hope into the next generation.

Empowering Future Kingdom Leaders

Each scholarship awarded is a step toward breaking cycles of poverty and building a new generation of educated, confident leaders for the Kingdom and society.

Play Video

Through generous donations, Beulah has already helped many children across Ghana stay in school and dream bigger dreams. Our goal is to increase the number of scholarships annually and reach more families who have sacrificed everything for the Gospel.

Program Highlights

Our Scholarships for Ministers' Children initiative includes:

  • Full or partial scholarships covering tuition fees
  • Provision of textbooks, uniforms, and supplies
  • Mentorship and academic encouragement
  • Support for primary, secondary, and tertiary education levels
  • Special awards for high-performing students

Supporting faithful Kingdom workers in rural Ghana through housing, education, and monthly care.
