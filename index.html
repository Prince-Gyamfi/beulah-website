About US

Remembering the Forgotten Ones in God's Kingdom

Beulah Foundation is a nonprofit organization committed to uplifting retired and active Kingdom workers serving in rural Ghana. Through housing, scholarships, and monthly support, we restore dignity to those who have faithfully labored in the vineyard of Christ.

Our Mission

To remember and restore the dignity of forgotten Kingdom workers

Our Vision

To build a thriving and dignified future for faithful Kingdom workers and their families,
