Evangelism and Rural Outreach

At Beulah Foundation, evangelism is at the heart of everything we do. Our Evangelism and Rural Outreach Program is dedicated to spreading the Gospel to unreached and underserved communities across Ghana. We support local ministers, mission teams, and churches to carry the message of hope to areas often neglected.

Through outreach missions, Bible distributions, and community gatherings, we bring the love of Christ to the forgotten corners of rural Ghana. Our outreach teams are equipped not just with words but also with tangible support such as food aid, clothing, and medical supplies.

Extending Light Where Darkness Remains

Many rural areas still lack consistent access to the Gospel. Through partnerships, training, and practical mission support, Beulah Foundation empowers Kingdom workers to reach the lost and disciple new believers.

Every soul reached through Beulah’s Evangelism and Rural Outreach is a testimony to God's unfailing love. As we partner together, the Kingdom expands — one village, one family, and one life at a time.

Program Highlights

Our Evangelism and Rural Outreach services include:

  • Organizing evangelistic crusades and rural outreaches
  • Providing Bibles and Gospel materials
  • Supporting rural pastors and local missionaries
  • Supplying humanitarian aid during outreach missions
  • Discipling new believers and supporting local churches

Supporting faithful Kingdom workers in rural Ghana through housing, education, and monthly care.
