Welfare & Relief Support

In times of unexpected hardship, faithful Kingdom workers often have nowhere to turn. Beulah Foundation’s Welfare & Relief Support program provides emergency help to pastors, their families, and Kingdom workers living in rural Ghana. We respond quickly to urgent needs for food, healthcare, clothing, and basic necessities.

Whether facing illness, displacement, loss, or financial crisis, no servant of God should struggle alone. Our Welfare Support ensures that those who once gave selflessly to others receive love, dignity, and real assistance when they need it most.

Responding with Compassion, Restoring with Care

Through our Welfare & Relief Support, we show Kingdom workers that they are seen, valued, and never forgotten — even in their darkest moments.

Play Video

Every donation toward Welfare Support goes directly to meeting critical needs — providing emergency medical care, food supplies, and essential household support. Together, we help lift burdens and remind Kingdom workers of God's faithful provision through His people.

Program Highlights

Our Welfare & Relief Support program offers:

  • Emergency food relief during times of crisis
  • Financial support for urgent medical care
  • Clothing and household essentials for families in need
  • Short-term housing assistance in emergencies
  • Ongoing pastoral care and counseling

Supporting faithful Kingdom workers in rural Ghana through housing, education, and monthly care.
